Alert

Provide contextual feedback messages for typical user actions with the handful of available and flexible alert messages. Alerts are available for any length of text, as well as an optional close button. For proper styling, use one of the eight required contextual classes (e.g., .alert-success).

Primary Alert!   I am a primary alert. Please check it out
secondary Alert!   I am an error alert. Please check it out.
Success Alert!   I am a success alert. Please check it out
Warning Alert!   I am a warning alert. Please check it out
